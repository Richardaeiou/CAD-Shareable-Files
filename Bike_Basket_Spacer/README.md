# Bike Basket Support

This support was created after I bought a handle mount basket for my Wife's new bike from a well known British chain store that sells bikes and car parts/accessories.  Unfortunately the handlerbar bracket was of very low quality, and made of plastic so if you tried to tighten it up to avoid the loaded basket rotating downwards and sitting on the front mudguard - the plastic just deformed!

I created this bracket to provide a spacer to keep the basket upright by resting on the head tube.

It was created in OpenScad to allow parameters to be altered to get it right - the STL file here suits our particular bike and basket, but the params allow you to modify it (to an extent) to suit by opening the scad file in OpenSCAD.
